Get involved

The Friends of Filnore Woods undertake management tasks at Filnore. We have regular work mornings from 10.00 am till 12.30 on the second Sunday and the fourth Wednesday of each month. We always need more helpers. Instruction and tools are provided. Join the emailing list at filnorefriends@gmail.com even if you only want to follow us.


Access for disabled people

Due to the uneven ground and sloping nature of the site, there is no wheelchair access and the site is not suitable for people with mobility problems. Contact us if you would like to discuss a 4x4 visit.
