Post 3


Three small-leaved lime trees planted here in 2002 commemorate the Queen ’s Golden Jubilee, the 750th anniversary of Thornbury ’s Town Charter, and the life of the Queen Mother, who died in March of that year. Across the path the mix of trees includes rowans and aspens with their trembling leaves.
