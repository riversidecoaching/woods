Post 7


Viewpoint. At 95m above sea level this is the highest point at Filnore. Viewing from left to right you can see: Marlwood School, Cassey Grove woodland, Thornbury golf course, the River Severn, the Forest of Dean, Oldbury-on-Severn Church, Oldbury nuclear power station, May Hill on the horizon, woods near Rockhampton, the Cotswold Edge and the Tyndale Monument at North Nibley.
