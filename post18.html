Post 18


This first large beech tree is about 150 years old and was probably planted as a boundary marker. From its shape you can see that it was once pollarded regularly to restrict its growth. The shade cast by beech trees means that not much can grow underneath.



Continue past the ruined cowshed concealed on the left, now a campfire circle, to the next beech tree, where you fork left



Turn right along the hedgeline
