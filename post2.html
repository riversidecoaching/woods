Post 2


In the south-west corner there is a group of trees including cherry, birch, hazel and oak. Straight ahead in the hedge is a mature beech.



Now turn left and climb the slope with the 1998 plantation on your left and the hedgeline on your right. Bramble is a good nest site for birds like long-tailed tits and whitethroats. Get your breath back on the bench at the top of the slope.
